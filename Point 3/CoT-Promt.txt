A farmer has 3 chickens, 2 pigs, and 1 cow. 
Each chicken has 2 legs, each pig has 4 legs, 
and each cow has 4 legs. How many legs are there in total?

Solve this problem step by step:

1. First, determine the number of legs for each type of animal.
2. Each chicken has 2 legs. How many legs do 3 chickens have?
3. Each pig has 4 legs. How many legs do 2 pigs have?
4. Each cow has 4 legs. How many legs does 1 cow have?
5. Now, add up the legs from all the chickens, pigs, and the cow.
What is the total number of legs?
